About Nitu Handicrafts

Rooted in craft tradition, driven by texture and form—Nitu Handicrafts brings macramé to modern homes.

Each piece is tied by hand, knot by knot, balancing strength with softness. We source cords with comfort in mind, and finish with care so your macramé sits beautifully for years.
